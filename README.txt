PHYSICAL COMPONENT -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This submission includes a physical component: a box containing 15 paper cards with QR codes.
These cards' QR codes are necessary for playing the game.
If you did not receive the physical component or do not have access to it, the printable A4 sheets of cards are included in the Cards folder at the root of this submission.
You can print these or just scan their QR codes directly from a photo viewer.
